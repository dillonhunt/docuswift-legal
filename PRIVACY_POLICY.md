# Privacy Policy for DocuSwift - File Converter

**Last updated: January 28, 2026**

## Introduction

DocuSwift - File Converter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Files You Convert
- Files you choose to convert are processed temporarily
- Files are never permanently stored on our servers
- Local conversions are processed entirely on your device
- Cloud conversions are processed via CloudConvert and immediately deleted after download

### Anonymous Usage Data
- We collect anonymous usage analytics via Firebase Analytics
- This includes app usage patterns, feature usage, and crash reports
- No personally identifiable information is collected
- This data helps us improve app functionality and stability

### Purchase Information
- In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
- We receive transaction confirmation but not your payment details
- Purchase history is stored by Apple, not by us
- We store your subscription status locally on your device

### Authentication Data
- We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication for cloud conversions
- No personal information (email, name, etc.) is required
- Anonymous user IDs are generated automatically
- These IDs cannot be traced back to you personally

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Perform file conversions (primary app function)
- Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Monitor app performance and fix bugs
- Provide customer support when requested

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)
- **Purpose**: Backend services, anonymous authentication, analytics
- **Data Shared**: Anonymous usage data, crash reports
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### CloudConvert
- **Purpose**: Cloud-based file conversion for complex formats
- **Data Shared**: Files you choose to convert
- **Data Retention**: Files are deleted immediately after conversion
- **Privacy Policy**: https://cloudconvert.com/privacy

### Apple StoreKit
- **Purpose**: In-app purchases and subscription management
- **Data Shared**: Purchase information (managed by Apple)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Converted files are stored temporarily in your device's local storage
- You control when files are deleted from your device
- No files are uploaded to our servers permanently

### Cloud Processing
- Files for cloud conversion are uploaded to CloudConvert via secure HTTPS
- Files are processed and immediately deleted after download
- We do not store or retain copies of your files

### Security Measures
- All network communications use HTTPS encryption
- Firebase authentication tokens are securely managed
- No plain-text storage of sensitive data

## Data Retention

- **Files**: Deleted immediately after conversion (cloud) or stored locally only (your device)
- **Analytics**: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months
- **Purchase History**: Managed by Apple's App Store
- **Anonymous Auth Tokens**: Expire after 60 days of inactivity

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we have collected
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (contact us)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics in device settings (Settings > Privacy > Analytics)
- **Control**: Delete files from your device at any time

## Children's Privacy

DocuSwift is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Users

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using DocuSwift, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.
